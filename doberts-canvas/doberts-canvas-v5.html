The Artist

Tim Dobert —
Fine Art Realist

As an artist deeply entrenched in the tradition of realism, I'm inspired by the timeless works of masters such as Rembrandt and Dürer. Based in the serene surroundings of Clifton Park in Saratoga County, my artistry seeks to capture the essence of life through a lens that emphasizes comfort, relaxation, and aspiration. My work is an exploration of the quiet moments that define our human experience, rendered with a meticulous attention to detail that pays homage to the great Realists who came before me.

In my artistic practice, I strive to create pieces that resonate with viewers on an emotional level, offering a visual sanctuary where the complexities of life are distilled into the simplicity of form and light. Each piece is a reflection of my pursuit to capture the beauty inherent in the everyday — transforming the ordinary into an aspirational vision of tranquility and introspection.

Through my work, I aim to evoke a sense of peace and aspiration, inviting viewers into the intricacies of my compositions. It is my hope that each viewer finds comfort in the familiar yet elevated depictions of life, as I continue to explore the intersections of reality and aspiration in my ongoing artistic journey.

Artist Statement

Why I Paint

I spent 25 years in Law Enforcement. I needed something to help me deal with the stress of work. So I started painting. When I retired I decided I wanted to devote my energies to my art — to create pieces that helped others escape the stress of daily life as well. Even if just for a short time.

— Tim Dobert, Dobert’s Canvas Fine Art Studio

Bespoke Artwork

Commission an
Original Painting

Tim accepts a small number of commissions each year. Every piece is painted in oils on canvas, crafted specifically for you, your space, and your vision.

The Process

Tim begins every commission with a conversation. You share your vision — subject, feeling, space — and he translates it into a composition. You'll receive progress updates and can refine before completion.

Turnaround

Most commissions are completed within 6–10 weeks depending on size and complexity. Rush timelines may be accommodated — please mention in your inquiry.

What's Included

Original oil on canvas, signed and varnished. Available framed or unframed. Certificate of authenticity included with every commissioned work.

Availability

Tim accepts limited commissions to ensure each receives full attention. Contact early to discuss your project and confirm availability for your timeline.
