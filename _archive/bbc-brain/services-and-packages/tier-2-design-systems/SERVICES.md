# Tier 2 — Design Systems & Custom Builds

Full custom web builds for businesses that need more than a starter site — bespoke design systems, complex interactions, and scalable architecture.

---

## Who This Is For

- Businesses with multiple product lines or service areas
- Brands that need a design system (not just a website)
- Projects requiring CMS, e-commerce, or custom integrations
- Clients who've outgrown a Tier 1 site

---

## What's Included

**Package D — Custom Site** ($5,000 – $8,000)
- Up to 10 pages
- Full design system (color, typography, components in Figma)
- Component-based Astro or Next.js build
- CMS integration (Sanity — client-editable content)
- SEO-optimized architecture
- 2 rounds of design revisions
- 30 days post-launch support
- Deploy to Netlify or Cloudflare Pages

**Package E — Custom + Commerce** ($8,000 – $12,000)
- Everything in Package D
- E-commerce or booking integration (Stripe, Square, Shopify storefront)
- Product or service catalog pages
- Order/inquiry management

**Package F — Brand + Build** ($7,500 – $11,000)
- Full brand identity (S1) + custom site build
- Logo, color system, typography, brand guidelines document
- Applied across all site components
- Cohesive brand rollout (social profile assets included)
