# Tier 3 — Ongoing Retainer

Monthly support for businesses that want their site to keep improving after launch — not go stale.

---

## Who This Is For

- Existing BBC clients (T1 or T2 builds)
- Businesses that update content regularly (menus, team, services)
- Clients who want a dedicated point of contact for all web needs
- Anyone who doesn't want to worry about maintenance

---

## What's Included

**Plan A — Maintenance** ($500/mo)
- Monthly security and dependency updates
- Uptime monitoring
- Performance check (Core Web Vitals)
- Up to 1 hour of minor content changes per month
- Priority support (48-hour response)

**Plan B — Growth** ($900/mo)
- Everything in Plan A
- Up to 3 hours of content updates per month
- Monthly SEO review (keyword tracking, meta optimization)
- Monthly analytics summary (GA4 + Search Console)
- One new page or feature per quarter (scoped per quarter)

**Plan C — Partner** ($1,500/mo)
- Everything in Plan B
- Up to 6 hours of updates/improvements per month
- Quarterly strategy session (what's working, what to build next)
- Priority build queue for new work
- Annual design refresh (update to current brand)

---

## Terms

- Month-to-month contracts after a 3-month minimum
- Cancel with 30 days notice
- Hours don't roll over (use them or lose them)
- Work beyond hours billed at $125/hr
- New features or pages outside retainer scope billed separately

---

## Retainer vs. Project Work

Retainer hours are for:
- Content changes (copy, photos, menu updates)
- Bug fixes and minor tweaks
- SEO maintenance and metadata
- Performance improvements

NOT for:
- New page designs from scratch → that's a T1 or T2 project
- Rebrand or redesign → project scope
- New integrations (booking system, payment) → project scope

If a retainer client request exceeds retainer scope, quote it as a project add-on.
