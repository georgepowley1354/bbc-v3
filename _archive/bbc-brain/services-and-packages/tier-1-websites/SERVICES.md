# Tier 1 — Websites

Starter custom website for businesses that need a professional web presence, built right, at an accessible price.

---

## Who This Is For

- Businesses launching their first real site
- Businesses replacing a DIY (Wix/Squarespace) site
- Clients who need fast, clean, and done — not complex

---

## What's Included

**Package A — Launch Site** ($2,500)
- Up to 4 pages (homepage, about, services, contact)
- Mobile-responsive design
- Contact form
- Deploy to Netlify or Cloudflare Pages
- Domain setup / DNS configuration
- 1 round of design revisions
- 2 weeks post-launch support

**Package B — Launch + Content** ($3,500)
- Everything in Package A
- Up to 6 pages
- Basic copywriting (we write the page copy based on discovery)
- SEO metadata for all pages

**Package C — Launch + Book** ($4,500)
- Everything in Package B
- Booking or reservation system integration (Calendly, OpenTable, or equivalent)
- Inquiry form with conditional logic
