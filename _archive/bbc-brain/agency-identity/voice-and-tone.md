# Voice and Tone

## Agency Voice Rules

These 6 rules govern how BBC communicates — in proposals, emails, briefs, and any client-facing writing.

**1. Plain English over agency speak**
Say "We'll build your website" not "We'll leverage our full-stack capabilities to architect a digital presence." If a client has to re-read a sentence, rewrite it.

**2. Confidence without hedging**
Say "This will load in under 2 seconds" not "We aim to achieve reasonable load times where possible." Own the claim or don't make it.

**3. Specificity over vagueness**
Say "The homepage, about page, services page, and contact form" not "several key pages." Clients decide with specifics, not with vague gestures.

**4. Short sentences, active voice**
BBC proposals read fast. No passive constructions, no 40-word sentences. If it sounds like a legal document, it's wrong.

**5. Results language in proposals, process language in SOWs**
Proposals answer "what will this do for you." SOWs answer "here's exactly what we're building." Don't mix the two registers.

**6. No unsolicited opinions on client choices**
If the client wants a carousel, build the best carousel possible. Voice opinions when asked, or when something will break. Not otherwise.

---

## Pitch Tone

When writing for proposals and pitches specifically:
- Lead with the client's problem, not BBC's credentials
- One short paragraph on what BBC does
- Then straight to the solution
- Avoid "we're excited to..." openers — just get to it

---

## What the Voice Is NOT

- Not casual to the point of unprofessional ("hey so here's the deal")
- Not corporate ("synergize deliverables across touchpoints")
- Not defensive or over-apologetic ("we would humbly suggest...")
- Not filler-heavy ("as a full-service agency with years of experience...")
- Not self-congratulatory — let the work speak, or reference past clients specifically

---

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid

| Wrong | Right |
|-------|-------|
| "We will endeavor to provide..." | "We'll deliver..." |
| "A bespoke digital experience" | "A custom website" |
| "Robust and scalable solution" | "A site that works now and can grow with you" |
| "Synergize your brand touchpoints" | "Make your website, social, and email feel like one thing" |
| "We're excited to partner with you" | Skip it. Just start with the work. |
