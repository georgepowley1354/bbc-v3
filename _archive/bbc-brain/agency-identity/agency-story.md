# Agency Story

## The Short Version

BBC builds websites for small businesses that need to look professional, load fast, and not require a tech degree to manage. Jorge started it because he kept seeing local businesses with terrible websites — or no website — that were otherwise doing great work. The gap wasn't ambition, it was access to a builder who'd actually execute without overcomplicating it.

---

## The Long Version

Most web agencies are built for either massive budgets or DIY. The expensive agencies come with project managers, brand strategists, and invoices that don't make sense for a restaurant with 12 tables. The DIY route means Wix sites that look generic and break when you need them most.

BBC sits in the middle intentionally. Jorge is a builder — he designs and codes. Clients don't get a handoff chain from "account manager to designer to developer." They work directly with the person building their site. That changes the communication dynamic: faster decisions, fewer misunderstandings, better output.

The work focuses on local and service businesses — restaurants, contractors, consultants, gyms, artists — businesses that need a web presence that works, not a brand manifesto.

---

## Positioning

**BBC is for:**
- Small businesses with serious intent (not hobbyists)
- Businesses that have outgrown their current site or don't have one
- Clients who want to work with a real person, not an agency hierarchy
- Projects with clear scope and a genuine launch date

**BBC is NOT for:**
- Enterprise companies that need teams of specialists
- Clients who want to "just see what we can come up with"
- Projects where the client is the designer and needs an executor
- Businesses that don't have a clear sense of what they're offering

---

## Brand Values

1. **Directness** — Say what the project is, what it costs, when it's done
2. **Craft** — Build things properly, not quickly-and-badly
3. **Clarity** — No jargon, no mystery, no scope creep by accident
4. **Reliability** — Show up, hit the milestone, handle the handoff

---

## The Long-Game Narrative

The goal isn't to grow BBC into a 20-person agency. It's to build a reputation for doing excellent work on a manageable number of projects per year. Referral-driven. Known in the local business community. The agency that small businesses call when they're ready to do it right.
