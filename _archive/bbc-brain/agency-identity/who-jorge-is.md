# Who Jorge Is

## One-Sentence Version

Jorge is a web designer and developer who builds fast, clean websites for small businesses — and doesn't make it complicated.

## Full Bio

Jorge runs BBC, a boutique web design and development agency. He's the kind of builder who'd rather ship a site that actually converts than argue about frameworks. He works primarily with local and service-based businesses — restaurants, contractors, consultants, gyms — and brings both design instinct and technical execution to every project.

BBC isn't an agency with a roster of account managers. Jorge is the person who takes the brief, designs the site, writes the code, and handles the handoff. Clients get direct access to the person building their thing.

## Background

- Web design and development, full-stack capable
- Specialty in Astro, Next.js, and component-based architecture
- Experience deploying to Netlify and Cloudflare Pages
- Works across branding, UX/UI design, and production builds
- Projects include: kris-mid-city (restaurant), whatsshakinbacon (food truck), remodelnow (contractor), doberts-canvas (artist), bbc-v3 (agency site)

## Credibility Rule

**Surface credentials through specificity, not claims.** Don't say "Jorge is an expert." Say "Jorge has built sites for restaurants, contractors, and local businesses — sites that load fast and don't need constant maintenance."

Specificity sells. Vague expertise claims don't.

## What Jorge Is NOT

- Not a freelancer who disappears after launch (he offers retainer support)
- Not a template shop (all builds are custom)
- Not a "big agency" experience — this is intentional
- Not focused on enterprise clients

## Working Style

- Direct communicator — says what he means, explains clearly
- Prefers scoped projects with clear deliverables over open-ended retainers
- Respects client time — no unnecessary meetings or status theater
- Gets into the details, but translates them plainly
