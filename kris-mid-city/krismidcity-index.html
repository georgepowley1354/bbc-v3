Our Story

Born from Behind the Bar

Kris spent years behind the bar before she had the chance to own one. When this Broadway spot came available, she took it — with a little help from a believer who saw what she saw.

That's how Mid City Tavern was born. A real neighborhood bar with a full kitchen, cold beers on tap, and the kind of food that makes you want to come back on a Wednesday just because.
