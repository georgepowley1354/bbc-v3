Common Questions

What Homeowners Ask Us

Do you handle permits?
Yes — we pull all necessary permits and manage the inspection process from start to finish. You won't have to deal with the town building department. We handle it.
How long does a typical remodel take?
It depends on scope. A bathroom remodel typically runs 1–2 weeks. A full kitchen gut-and-rebuild is usually 3–5 weeks. We set a realistic timeline before we start and stick to it — our customers regularly note we finish early.
Do you use subcontractors?
No. Our own crew handles every trade — carpentry, tile, plumbing rough-in, and more. The same trusted team shows up every morning and cleans up every evening. No strangers cycling through your home.
Can you handle multiple projects at once?
Yes. We've completed full kitchen and bathroom renovations simultaneously for the same homeowner. Our crew is sized to handle multi-scope projects efficiently, and Carl or Paul is on site to coordinate every day.
Do you clean up daily?
Every evening. We don't leave a job site messy overnight. Tools are packed, debris is contained, and your home is livable while we work — that's a standard part of how we operate.
